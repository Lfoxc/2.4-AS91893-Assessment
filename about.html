DITCS2 is a schoolwide organization tasked with helping students taking digital tech in terms of revising computer science concepts, students who wish to know more about these topics and teachers who are looking for a simple way to teach computer science topics to their students as well as educate themselves. This website includes examples and types of computer science topics such as Encryption, error control, and computer security, and potentially more, and the opportunity to try these concepts out for themselves using the interactives provided with each topic. DITCS2 stands for Digital technologies computer science (NCEA) level 2, because the website currently focuses only on NCEA level 2 external subjects for digital technologies.
In the future this website will focus more on catering to the needs of all digital tech students from all NCEA levels. As of now, you can find links to the NCEA level 1 and 3 pages at the bottom of the page (footer), with a brief overview of the subjects that will be covered.
