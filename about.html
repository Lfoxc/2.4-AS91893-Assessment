About DITCS

Image by Gerd Altmann from Pixabay.

DITCS is a schoolwide organization tasked with helping students taking digital tech in terms of revising computer science concepts, students who wish to know more about these topics and teachers who are looking for a simple way to teach computer science topics to their students as well as educate themselves. DITCS stands for Digital technologies computer science (NCEA), because the website focuses on all external subjects for digital technologies.

In the future this website will focus more on catering to the needs of all digital tech students from all NCEA levels.
